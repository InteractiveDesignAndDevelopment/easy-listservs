Lookup Class Listerservs

Original Page

  • Enter the Course ID and Section (e.g. BIO101) to look up.
  • Click the Generate Lookup Email button.
  • A pre-formatted email will be created in your default email client.
  • Send that email.
  • You will receive an email listing matching classes.
  • Find your class in the list and add @lists.mercer.edu (e.g. BIO101A2111SPMCN00@lists.mercer.edu).
  • Only people subscribed to the list will be allowed to send messages to it.
  • No subscriptions from non-Mercer e-mail accounts will be allowed.
