Instructions on using this Lookup Tool Enter the Course ID and Section for the requested class listserv in the field below. After submitting the form, you will receive an email with a list of all of the class lists that begin with the course and section number you entered. To email your class, find your course in the list and add @lists.mercer.edu (e.g. BIO101A2111SPMCN00@lists.mercer.edu). Only those people subscribed to the list will be allowed to send messages to it. No subscriptions from non-Mercer e-mail accounts will be allowed.
